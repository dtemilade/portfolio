About me

Full-Stack - - Web Dev.

I am Temilade Dauda, a passionate and dedicated software developer with a solid foundation in Computer Science and a growing track record of professional achievements in the tech industry. My academic journey began at Lagos State Polytechnic, Ikorodu, Lagos, where I earned both my National Diploma (ND) and Higher National Diploma (HND) in Computer Science.

To improve my skills, I also completed a Software Engineering (Backend) program with ALX Africa, learning advanced coding, backend development, databases, and software architecture.

In addition to my software engineering training, I hold an International Certification as an Adobe Certified Professional (ACP) in Web Authoring using Dreamweaver. This credential validates my expertise in web design, user interface development, responsive web layouts, and standards-based web authoring, enhancing my versatility as a developer.

Alongside my academic pursuits, I am a proficient minstrel and hold a deep devotion to spiritual matters. Additionally, I dedicate my time as a volunteer contributor to the annual devotional book "Food for the Journey," where I actively participate in its creation and dissemination.

Over time, I’ve worked on different projects and other web-based tools. I am not only committed to writing clean and efficient code but also to continuous learning and staying current with industry best practices. I enjoy solving problems, helping create useful technology and working in environments that encourage growth and creativity.

Beyond technical skills, I bring strong communication, project management, and leadership qualities, cultivated through professional experience and volunteer work. I am driven by excellence, integrity, and a desire to create solutions that make a positive impact.
